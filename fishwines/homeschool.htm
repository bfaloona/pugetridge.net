Homeschool: 7th Grade on the Road

 

 

Annie is spending 7th Grade on the road. The world will be her classroom. Annie spent 6th grade at the Northwest School and hopes to return there for 8th grade. The core of her curriculum will be observing and living in many new cultures, continuing her study of Spanish and music (piano and recorder), keeping a daily journal, nightly family reading and of course, doing her beloved math problems (including keeping track of all the new money we've got to deal with!).

Posted on this page down the road, you may find some of Annie's writing and other interesting assignments.
