India Pictures, Feb-Mar 2003 / 24kolam - cow

4/6/2003

Kolam - cow - We were taken to a small village outside Gandhigram by Pappu a wonderful man and project manager. There, we were treated to a wonderful Kolam drawing demonstration and lunch. Here Pappu is pulling a cow around the Kolam so that we can take a picture before it's ruined - the destiny of all Kolams.
