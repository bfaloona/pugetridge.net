India Pictures, Feb-Mar 2003 / 09Sweetie & SHG

4/6/2003

Sweetie and SHG - Self-Help Groups of Village women are being organized all over Tamil Nadu. In the village we stayed there were a number of groups. Each group has about 15 or so women who share money together for a loan program, complete projects for the betterment of the village and support struggling families. Sweetie (yes that's really her name) is in the foreground, a future woman leader.
