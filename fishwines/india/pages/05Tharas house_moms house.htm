India Pictures, Feb-Mar 2003 / 05 Tharas house & moms house

4/6/2003

Thara's house and mom's house - The house where Barbara and Annie stayed for about 2 weeks along with Thara and her two daughters (Durga and Tainmarie) with the women sitting on the stoop. Thara's mother's house is the thatch roofed one on the right. The porch under the overhang was a wonderful place to visit and avoid the heat of the day. Notice the drainage trench in front of the houses. Storm water and drains emptied into this trench. You can also see the Hindu shrine at the end of the street.
