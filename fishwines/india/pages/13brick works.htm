India Pictures, Feb-Mar 2003 / 13brick works

4/6/2003

Brick works - On the edge of Edayansathu, people make bricks from local clay. Here a batch is cooling while another is being fired. The bricks are piled up in a truncated cone shape and packed with cow dung and straw and burned for a few days. The bricks are used to build houses and covered with cement plaster.
