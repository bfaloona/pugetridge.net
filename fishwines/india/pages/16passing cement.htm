India Pictures, Feb-Mar 2003 / 16passing cement

4/6/2003

Passing cement - while we were in Edayansathu village, a new road was being built. Under the Panchayat system, the village prioritizes their needs and the government provides the funding for projects (after a great deal of pressure). Here are village women passing bowls of concrete from the mixer to the leading edge of the pour. Village women are paid about 75 rupees a day ($1.50) for their work. The pour will last two days and everyone is happy: Self-Help village improvement and employment.
