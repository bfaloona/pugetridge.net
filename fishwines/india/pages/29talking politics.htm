India Pictures, Feb-Mar 2003 / 29 talking politics

4/6/2003

Talking politics - on the floor of Ramalingam's living/sleeping/dining room one day, we had a wonderful visit with Ramalingam's father: KM Natarajan (right of Paul) and a number of his friends involved in organic agriculture. The man facing the camera is an ex-scientist who has written a book suggesting Eastern revisions to Marxist thought, believing that Marxism's failure was not recognizing love. Notice Annie on the left spinning cotton on the drop spindle she got in Ethiopia.
