India Pictures, Feb-Mar 2003 / 17Murugesan + Rani + B

4/6/2003

Murugesan, Rani and B - the driving forces behind much of the positive change in Edayansathu village are Rani (left) who gained confidence and experience in Self-Help Groups and eventually was elected to be the president of her village panchayat; and Murugesan, the project Director of SHARE which has created SHG's with over 3,000 women in over 50 villages. We spent much time with these two and their families. We were well taken care of! We were also able to contribute some by photographing all of the craft products, designing a web site for SHARE and putting together a nomination of Rani for best Panchayat President in India.
