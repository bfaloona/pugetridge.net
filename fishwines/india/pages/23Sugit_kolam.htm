India Pictures, Feb-Mar 2003 / 23 Sugit & kolam

4/6/2003

Sugit and Kolam - Tamil women each morning, sweep the ground in front of their homes and create these beautiful designs. These designs only last a day at most as daily life crosses them from the moment they are drawn. We stayed at this house with our SERVAS HOSTS Poopathi and Suganthi and their son Sugit in the photo.
