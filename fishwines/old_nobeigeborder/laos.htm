We know very little about Laos, but off we go. We decided to include Laos in our trip only recently on the advice of a friend, "You should go to Laos." Looking at a map, we saw that we could travel overland from Thailand, through Laos and into Yunan Province. This also helped us pin down where to go in China.



From South India, we fly into Bankok and then to Chang Mai in the north of Thailand. From there we travel by overland into Laos and by boat down the Mekong River. We have heard that there are villages accessible only by boat on the Mekong and there we hope to find a place to stay for a month.
