Pakistan has been in the news a lot over the past year...


 

...With the U.S. chasing Osama bin Ladin into the caves of Afghanistan bordering Pakistan on the west and with heightened tension along the eastern border with India, Pakistan has been at the center of many storms. But this is not why we're going to Pakistan.

Our good friends Lali and Khalid live in Karachi. Around 11 years ago, we met Lali and Khalid who were living in the north end of Seattle at the time and we each had a one year old - Annie and Rahul, who were both learning to walk. We would spend whole days together eating, playing with the children and sharing their lives. Not long after Ravi was born, Lali and Khalid moved back to Pakistan to be closer to family and pursue career moves that were not possible here in the states. We miss them terribly and plan to make a short stop in Pakistan to visit them.

We hope that tension subsides in Pakistan so that people may live in peace - particularly our good friends there. We may only be in Pakistan for 10 days but we hope to be able to share some thoughts and observations of what we notice.
