Italy Pictures, Oct. 2002 / 13Sienna from the tower

11/19/2002

Sienna's wealthy merchant families (9 of them) in the 12th century (?) built a municipal building that rivaled the church in scale on an enormous public square with a bell tower. We climbed up the tower (as most tourists do) to see the panorama. Note the abrupt edge where tightly packed urban environment gives way to forest and farm - good idea!
