Italy Pictures, Oct. 2002 / 11Pompeii

11/19/2002

This is not the best view of Pompeii but it shows Mount Visuvius in the back ground which buried this Roman town in volcanic ash and mud in A.D. 76 preserving many of its residential, civic, and religious buildings. This view looks across the forum, the heart of the city where politics and commerce took place and I'll bet a lot of "seeing and being seen."
