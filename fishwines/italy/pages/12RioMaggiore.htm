Italy Pictures, Oct. 2002 / 12RioMaggiore

11/19/2002

Look at the sixth building over from the left (the yellow one) with the open shutters three floors up from the stone arch - the window with the laundry - that was ours for 3 days in this 700 year old town in Italy's Cinque Terra coast. We ate, played cards, wrote haiku and hung out our laundry with the Camille, Julien, Rod, and Veronique.
