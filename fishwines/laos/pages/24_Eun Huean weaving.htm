Eun Huean weaving
Phouy’s daughter Eun Huean (15) set up their loom and taught Annie to weave the intricate Lao patterned fabric during our stay there. The pattern is initially set up manually and “recorded” with a series of sticks that are moved back and forth above and below the fabric threads to reproduce the pattern. (hard to explain, but it works). Here’s Eun Huean setting up the initial pattern
