5/14/2003

La Cooking
The kitchen was a simple one with a small wood fire on a dirt floor, but La turned out incredible, healthy food with seemingly little effort. The kitchen was on the ground level “under the house” – this is also where we ate all our meals and hung out (upstairs was just for sleeping). Phouy’s house is a “stilt house” like all others in the village, but Phouy has enclosed his ground floor with woven bamboo walls.
