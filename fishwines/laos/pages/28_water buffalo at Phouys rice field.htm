Water buffalo at Phouy’s rice field
Many animals in town – especially chickens, ducks, dogs, cats and rats. But the strangest for us were the water buffalo, which plough the fields just before the rainy season (and sometimes end up on the dinner table). Here’s the small herd near Phouy’s rice field.
