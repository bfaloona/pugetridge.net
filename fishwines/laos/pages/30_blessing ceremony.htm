Blessing ceremony
Although Ban Soupjem has a small Buddhist temple, most of the people have strong animist leanings. Animists believe that everything around us has a spirit. Their “blessing ceremony” is used to give special blessings to honor someone or help someone in need (sick child, etc). During this ceremony, everyone ties simple cotton strings around the wrists of the person being honored as they offer their blessings.
