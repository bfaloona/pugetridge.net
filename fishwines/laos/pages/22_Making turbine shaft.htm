Making turbine shaft
Phouy loved to find “a better way” and was installing this “new improved” version of a turbine that channeled its output water through a large pipe. Problem is that there are no large pipes in a village like this. So what do you do? Hollow out a log of course. Paul’s helping Phouy put the log pipe back together here in hopes of more electricity.
