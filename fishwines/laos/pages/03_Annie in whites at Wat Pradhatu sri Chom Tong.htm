5/14/2003

Annie in white at Wat Pradhatu sri Chomthong
We did a four-day meditation retreat at Wat Pradhatu sri Chomthong, just outside of Chiang Mai, Thailand. We learned about insight meditation and each took a deeper look into ourselves. During the retreat, we wore white every day – here’s Annie in her whites (with kitty!).
