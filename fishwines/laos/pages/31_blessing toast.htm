Blessing toast
Oh, did we mention that drinking is also a part of this ceremony??? Well, yes, it is, both the whiskey-like Lao Lao and the local Lao beer (usually alternated with each toast). Here’s Paul toasting with Phouy and “Broken Hand”, Phouy’s best friend who lost his hand setting off explosives in the river to “tease out” the fish.
