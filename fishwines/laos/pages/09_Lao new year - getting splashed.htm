Lao New Year – getting splashed
We were in Luang Prabang for the Lao New Year celebration. It’s considered “the” place to be in Laos, so of course there were many other tourists there with us. Part of the celebration includes splashing water on people as they walk or drive down the street (sometimes buckets of water!). Here’s Paul and Annie getting the 50th splash of the day – a welcome treat actually since this is the hottest month in Laos.
