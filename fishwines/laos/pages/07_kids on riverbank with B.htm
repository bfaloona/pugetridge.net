5/14/2003

Kids on riverbank with B
Mid-way down the Mekong, we stopped at Pak Bang and went inland for a couple days to visit the small town of Muang Houn. Paul and Annie swam in a river outside of town to the amazement of the children in the area. Here they are, fascinated but keeping a respectable distance from Barbara as Paul takes the photo from the river.
