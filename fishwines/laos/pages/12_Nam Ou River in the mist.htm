Nam Ou River in the mist
We enlisted the help of a guide to locate a small fishing village to spend a few weeks. He understood well what we wanted and took us up the Nam Ou River from Muang Ngoi to the village of Ban Soupjem. The Nam Ou is a beautiful, slow river sometimes shrouded in mist.
