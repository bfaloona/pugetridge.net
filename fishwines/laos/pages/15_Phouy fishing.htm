Laos Pictures, April 2003 / 15_Phouy fishing

5/14/2003

Phouy fishing
Ban Soupjem is a subsistence fishing/farming village and we landed here at the home of Phouy and La and their family. Phouy and Paul struck up a great friendship and Phouy took Paul out fishing most every evening (to lay the nets) and every morning (to pull the nets in). Paul was probably more company than help, since as you can see in this photo, Phouy is capable of precariously balancing at the end of his narrow boat to both lay the net and paddle the boat at the same time.
