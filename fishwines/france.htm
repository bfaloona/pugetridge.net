France will be the site of an amazing friendship reunion. Annie's two best friends in Cohousing will be in France while we are on our journey and we will make it there by October 7th, Annie's 13th birthday. There will undoubtedly be a sleepover involved.


 

We plan to stay with friends in Lyon for a few weeks and take a few side trips biking into the French countryside. Then together with our Cohousing friends living in France, we will take off down to Italy to visit some of the northern Italian hill towns. After a few weeks, we will part as they return to Lyon and we head down to Brendisi - on the east coast of the heal of the boot. There, we will pick up a boat bound for Greece and eventually on to Turkey.
