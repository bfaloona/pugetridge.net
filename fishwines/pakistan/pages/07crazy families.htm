Karachi, Pakistan Pictures, Jan. 2003 / 07crazy families

1/25/2003

The purpose of our visit to Karachi was to see our great friends, the Mahmood family (Lali, Khalid, Rahul and Ravi) who lived in Seattle for a number of years before moving back to Pakistan. We routinely stayed up to way past midnight, talking, telling bad jokes, making music, playing backgammon and just plain goofing around.
