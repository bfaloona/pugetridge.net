Dubai, Jan. 2003 / 01skyline

1/25/2003



Dubai is an amazing city in the United Arab Emirates. The City has been created as a shopping and leisure Mecca. It's got glitzier shopping malls and fancier hotels than you could ever see in one U.S. City. It's also got an amazing cultural diversity that rivals the U.S. without the white majority and the people who live their, from India, Pakistan, the Arabian Peninsula, the Philippines…dress as much in traditional clothes as they do in Western clothe.
