Karachi, Pakistan Pictures, Jan. 2003 / 17Old City street scene

1/25/2003

Because of recent tensions in Pakistan, most of our time in Karachi was spent inside. One of the trips we took though was a drive through the Old part of Karachi. It was an incredible mix of historic colonial architecture, makeshift housing, lots of people and animals, boys playing cricket in the streets, traffic, garbage…you name it.
