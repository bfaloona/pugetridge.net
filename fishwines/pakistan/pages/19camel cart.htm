1/25/2003

Karachi's traffic problems are different than ours. Of course, like us, they suffer at times from too many cars for the surface of road. They also have the problem of serious potholes and piles of rubble that need to be swerved around. Donkeys, Camels, three wheeled motor carts, scooters, cars and busses all share the road. As a consequence of all this, no one really pays any attention to lanes. Here's a picture out the back windshield of a camel cart.
