Ethiopia Pictures, Jan. 2003 / 01Addis bus station

2/8/2003

I think you have to be there to fully appreciate the intensity, crowds, confusion, and craziness of this predawn Addis Ababa bus station scene. But here it is on the day we left Addis & we managed to survive it (mostly because they take pity on tourists and let us go first). Why, oh why do all the buses leave at 6 in the morning???
