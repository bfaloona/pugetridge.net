Ethiopia Pictures, Jan. 2003 / 03Blue Nile Falls

2/8/2003

We hiked to see the famous Blue Nile Falls just outside the city of Barhar Dar. The falls are beautiful, though considerably smaller than shown in most Ethiopia posters, since ¾ of the water has now been diverted to generate electricity. We met this boy at the falls - he taught us how to make a leaf whistle and we taught him how to make a blade of grass whistle.
