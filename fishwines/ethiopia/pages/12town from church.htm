Ethiopia Pictures, Jan. 2003 / 12town from church

2/8/2003

Here's a view looking down onto the main street of Debebuhar from the hill on the way up to the church. (Did we mention it was a small town??) We lived just outside this town for a week - the guests of Kesi Adamasi, the local priest and uncle of Paul's friend Tigabie in Seattle. The street is the main road heading north-south through Ethiopia but the village has no electricity, phone service, sewers, etc. You can see the dust rising off the road.
