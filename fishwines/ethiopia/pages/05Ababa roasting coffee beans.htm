Ethiopia Pictures, Jan. 2003 / 05Ababa roasting coffee beans

2/8/2003

The coffee ceremony is Ethiopia's cultural tradition, starting with roasting of the beans, hand grinding them and heating over a charcoal fire. Papyrus grass laid on the floor and colorful incense bowl are also traditional. Three cups are always served, "abol, tona and baraka." Ababa ("flower") was the sister of a young boy we met in the market who invited us to his home.
