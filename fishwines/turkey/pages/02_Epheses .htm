Turkey Pictures, Nov. 2002 / 02_Epheses

11/26/2002

The old Roman ruins of Ephesus (once a seaport, but now silt deposits place it 5 km from the sea) can be viewed near Selcuk. The old amphitheater is shown here with modern-day Annie and Paul. This trip immerses us in the historic overlays of Roman/Greek/Arabic cultures and Christian/Muslim religions.
