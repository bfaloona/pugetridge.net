Turkey Pictures, Nov. 2002 / 16_Kalburlu Church interior

11/26/2002

Every detail of this interior has been carved from a single rock - columns, arches, domed ceilings. And this is just one of 50 in addition to many other just plane old rooms cut into the rock. We were the only ones hiking around looking at these churches - no tourists, no ice cream, no junk shops. There were cattle, sheep and a few farmers and children we saw nearby.
