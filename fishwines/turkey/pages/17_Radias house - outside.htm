Turkey Pictures 2, Dec. 2002 / 17_Radias house - outside

12/22/2002

This is the house we're staying in here in Guzelyurt with Radiya, her brother Murat and their mother "mommy". The house is made of stone with vaulted ceilings and arched windows - all of stone. Part of this wall is carved into the bedrock, some is rough stone masonry, some is finely carved with beautiful detail. Behind the wall, you find courtyards, rubble, gardens, cows, sheep, ruins, etc. The door to Radiya's courtyard is the farthest one. Notice the grape vines hanging over.
