Turkey Pictures, Nov. 2002 / 13_Ramazan Feast

11/26/2002

We arrived in Turkey during the month of Ramazan. During Ramazan, Muslim families fast from sunrise to sundown and end the fast each day with a family meal. "Turkish hospitality" has given us new friends and included us in several of these warm events. Here we are with family and friends of Mehmet, principal of the local high school.
