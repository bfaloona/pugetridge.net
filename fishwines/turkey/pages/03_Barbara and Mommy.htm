Turkey Pictures, Nov. 2002 / 03_Barbara and Mommy

11/26/2002

As we walked down a Selcuk street, "Mommy" called us from her balcony and invited us up for Turkish coffee, olives, chai (tea) and oranges. We joined her and her family on the rooftop overlooking Selcuk and it's Roman era castle walls. Barbara bought this scarf that she had crocheted.
