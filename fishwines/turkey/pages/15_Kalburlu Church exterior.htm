Turkey Pictures, Nov. 2002 / 15_Kalburlu Church exterior

11/26/2002

Leaving from the center of Guzelyurt, we turn right and walk into "Monestary Vally" which has over 50 churches literally cut into the rock within a 3 mile hike. Here's a look from the outside. On the far right of this photo you can see the upper right hand corner of the doorway that steps back into the Kalburlu church (see the next photo for the interior).
