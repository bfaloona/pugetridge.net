Turkey Pictures, Nov. 2002 / 01_We made it

11/26/2002

After a marathon of buses and ferries across Greece, we made it to Turkey on the "last boat of the season" from Samos. We traveled up the coast of Turkey to the town of Selcuk, near Ephesus, and landed at "Jimmy's Place", a guesthouse ($5/person/night). Here's Annie and Barbara hanging out around the fire in Jimmy's restaurant.
