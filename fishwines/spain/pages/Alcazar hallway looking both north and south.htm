10/15/2002

Alcazar hallway looking both north and south
The subtle colors of Sevilla - here's a hallway in the Alcazar (castle in Sevilla) first looking south (left) down it and then looking north (right) down the same hall (all daylight - no electric). The subtle change of color was real, and I'm still trying to figure out what mysterious play of light caused it.
