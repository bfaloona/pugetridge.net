Spain, September, 2002 / Sevilla from top of Giralda Tower

Posted: 9/24/2002

Sevilla from top of Giralda Tower:
From the top of the Giralda Tower, we got views around all of Sevilla. All of the town center has narrow twisting streets and many plazas. We have been lost here many times - a map is essential!! Here's a photo that shows one of the plazas and one of the major (but still narrow and curving) streets leading off it.
