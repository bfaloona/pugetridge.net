Fishwine Update 3 / Calle El Chorro

10/15/2002

Calle El Chorro
After trying the one small hotel in the center of El Bosque (too expensive and not available for two days), we tried the Rick Steves approach (just ask in one of the local bars who has a place to rent). This led us down the street to the Beauty parlor , no luck. Then around the corner to the pastry shop, no luck there either but the women behind the counter called her brother who knew someone, who knew someone else. Half a dozen phone calls later, we rented a small casita (house) on this street. This is a typical streetscape in town.
