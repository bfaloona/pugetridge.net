Fishwine Update 3 / Teresa, P and AJ in our casita

10/15/2002

Teresa, P & AJ in our casita
Most days in El Bosque, we spent some time with Teresa, either in the plaza or in her house, or the house of her son, Pedro. We talked a bit and sat a bit and just passed the time together. The night before we left, Teresa surprised us by coming to our house to say "goodbye." Here she is in our living room with Paul and Annie - you can see her warm spirit showing through!
