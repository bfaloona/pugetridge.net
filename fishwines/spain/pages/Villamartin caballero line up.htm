Fishwine Update 3 / Villamartin caballero line up

10/15/2002

Villamartin caballero lineup
On our way to El Bosque, we found ourselves in Villamartin for a couple of hours between buses. Luckily for us there was a fair happening - a celebration of flamenco, drinking and dancing horses. Here's a lineup of caballeros getting ready to parade around the fairgrounds.
