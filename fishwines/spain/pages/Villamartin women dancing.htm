Fishwine Update 3 / Villamartin women dancing

10/15/2002

Villamartin women dancing
It seemed like every woman in town over 8 was dressed for flamenco at the fair - we were too early for most of the dancing, but the dresses were wonderful. As we climbed the hill to catch our bus, there was a continual flood of women in these colorful dresses heading toward the fair.
