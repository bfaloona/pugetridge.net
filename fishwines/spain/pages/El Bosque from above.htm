Fishwine Update 3 / El Bosque from above

10/15/2002

El Bosque from above
El Bosque is one of the smaller Pueblos Blancos (white hill towns) in Spain, nestled into the foothills of the Sierra de Grazalema. Here's a photo of the town from the hills above it - at the edge of town (2/3 of the way up on the photo) you can see its bullring (no town is complete without one, but, no, we haven't seen a bullfight - except on TV)
