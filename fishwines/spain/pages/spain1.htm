Spain, September, 2002 / Lucas, Roberto y Lucas in Sevilla

Posted: 9/24/2002

 

Lucas, Roberto y Lucas in Sevilla:
In Sevilla, we have landed in the home (third floor apartment) of Pilar and Lucas and their three kids, Lucas, Roberto and Pilar and their trusty dog, Morro. Here's a photo of Lucas, Roberto and Lucas taken after the big lunch meal, which we ate with them every day (and put back some of the weight we lost in Todos Santos). Lucas (Sr.) is a photographer. He doesn't like digital photos but we had fun with him fooling with our digital camera and displaying them on the second hand laptop we bought on Ebay the week before we left.
