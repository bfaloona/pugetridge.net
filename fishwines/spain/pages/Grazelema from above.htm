Fishwine Update 3 / Grazelema from above

10/15/2002

Grazalema from above
One day we took the bus from El Bosque to Grazalema at 6:45 am. We were the only people on the bus, so we asked the driver to let us off about a kilometer before town and walked in. It was just before sunrise & a wonderful, mystical way to enter town, walking down the hill. After coffee in town, we climbed a peak above the city and photo'd the town from above. Here's a view of its jumble of red tile roofs and white buildings.
