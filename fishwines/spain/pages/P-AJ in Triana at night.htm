Fishwine Update 3 / P-AJ in Triana at night

10/15/2002

P & AJ in Triana at night
Sevilla comes alive at night, especially the Triana neighborhood. Everyone takes to the streets - talking, drinking, eating (tapas). Here's Paul and Annie on our last night in Sevilla, beside the river in Triana, bright city lights sparkle above them in the background.
