Posted: 9/24/2002

Narrow streets in barrio Sta. Cruz
The Barrio Santa Cruz in Sevilla is famous for its narrow, twisting streets - some of them only 4 or 5 feet wide - most of them for pedestrians and motorcycles only, but the wider ones (10-12') for cars also!! It's beautifully picturesque (and very touristy) and impossible to capture in one photo. Our language school was in this neighborhood.
