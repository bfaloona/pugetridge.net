10/15/2002

Quick friends in Villamartin
While Barbara was snapping photos at the fair, she was accosted by this group of men who wanted their pictures taken. She took their photo; then we all stood around talking for a while & by the time we were finished they had invited us for a drink and demanded a photo all together. We declined the drink, but got the photo. (Note, the guy in the sunglasses is either an important politician or a policeman, or both - our Spanish wasn't that good at this point, so we figured it was a good idea to do what he wanted!)
