Guatemala, August 2002 / Amparo with pan

Posted: 9/6/2002

Amparo with pan
Amparo is the mother of the household we’re living with in Todos Santos (although her eldest daughter, Flori, seems to be the heart of the family now).  Ampara bakes the bread for the family – here she is with her beautiful and bounteous bread in one of the three kitchens in the house before it’s baked.
