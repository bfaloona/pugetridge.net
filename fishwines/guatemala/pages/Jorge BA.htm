Guatemala, August 2002 / Jorge B&A

Posted: 9/6/2002

Annie, Jorge & Barbara
Jorge was a wonderful Mam man living in Todos Santos.  He was Barbara and Annie’s Spanish teacher for three weeks.  Jorge was one of the first indigenous schoolteachers in the highlands of Guatemala and has retired after 20 years of teaching kids.  He took a lot of harassment in his early days of breaking a racist barrier and has paved the way for many other indigenous people who have since become school teachers.  In the local school in Todos Santos, most kids speak Mam as their first language and indigenous teachers can teach both English and Spanish, helping to keep the culture alive.
