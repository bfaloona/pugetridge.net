Guatemala, August 2002 / Market day 6

Posted: 9/6/2002

Market day 6
Saturday is market day in Todos Santos and it gets packed!  This is the main street in town.  Folks from the surrounding hills come to town to sell their weavings, produce and assorted pots, pans and clothes.  The local indigenous people are the Mam.  Their colorful characteristic clothing (for both men and women) adds wonderful color and interest to the street scenes.
