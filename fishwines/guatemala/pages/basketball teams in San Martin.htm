Guatemala, August 2002 / basketball teams in San Martin

Posted: 9/6/2002

Basketball teams in San Martin
One weekend, we went with Magdali (13 year old girl in the family we lived with in Todos Santos) to meet Estella in her hometown of San Martin.  Estella is an indigenous woman who works for the family we live with (cooking, cleaning, etc.).  San Martin is a small, very poor mountain town about an hour from Todos Santos by bus.  At the basketball court in the center of town, near the church, Magdali suggested that she, Barbara, Annie, and I challenge these boys to a basketball game.  We did.  We lost.
