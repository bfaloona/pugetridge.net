Guatemala, August 2002 / baking in the kitchen

Posted: 9/6/2002

Baking in the kitchen
Here is Flori baking in the kitchen.  Otto is in the front and Magdali is sitting near the wood-burning cook stove.  Estella is in the far back and Amparo is the other women in the picture.  I think you can see the friendly warmth in Flori’s eyes and smile.  She, along with all of the other women we say worked very hard cooking, cleaning, and caring for the home and all of its inhabitants.
