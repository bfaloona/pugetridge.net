Guatemala, August 2002 / annie-marcella weaving 2

Posted: 9/6/2002

Annie Marcella Weaving 2
Annie’s now weaving after much preparation.  Walking around any mountain Pueblo, you can see women in front of their houses weaving like this.  Notice the wood pile (for cooking) and the Adobe of Marcella’s house in the background.  Marcella’s house is completely surrounded by corn plants.
