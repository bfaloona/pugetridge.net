From Ethiopia, we plan to fly to India. Once we arrive in Madras (Chennai), we plan to take the train to the south of India eventually arriving in Madurai, a 2500 year old City of 1.2 million people in the State of Tamil Nadu known for its elaborate temples that are still very much part of Tamil culture. We have connected with a few Servas host families in the smaller district capitals surrounding Madurai, including Dindigul, Gandhigram and Rajapalaiyam.

We hope to stay a few days with some of these families so that we can find our bearings and perhaps make connections for an extended stay in a rural Tamil village during February/March.
