Spain (with a population about the same as Seattle) will be one of the few places on our trip where we expect to settle comfortably into first-world comforts in an urban neighborhood: our natural habitat. By that time, we will have spent a month in Guatemala, another Spanish speaking country (although I've heard people talk fast in Southern Spain and it can feel like a very different Spanish). This is also one place we hope to be able to set Annie up with piano lessons - one of her passions.
